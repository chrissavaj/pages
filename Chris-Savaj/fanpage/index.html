About Me

Hi, I'm Chris Savaj, a police officer based in Germany, and also a passionate actor

I’m from Cologne and I am currently working as a Police Officer in a station located in Mülheim.

I’ve served in the police force for over 8 years now. For me, it’s more than a job. it's a commitment to safety justice, and standing up for what is right in my community.

Outside the uniform, I enjoy acting. I have taken part in a few films and TV projects here in Germany, often bringing real-life experience into roles I play. Acting allows me to show a different side of myself and tell stories that reflect the world we live in.

Whether I’m in duty or on set, I bring discipline, heart, and purpose to everything I do
